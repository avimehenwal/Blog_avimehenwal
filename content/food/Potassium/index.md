# Electrolytes

* *Na+ / K+ Pumps*
* Almost 1/3rd of all food energy goes to charge Na+/K+ pumps
* Every cell in our body requires K to function.

## Potassium rich foods

1. Pinto beans or any light coloured beans
2. Spinache
3. Squashes
4. Avacado
5. Banana (300 mg of K)
6. Mushrooms

## Greens

1. Spinache - leutene
2. Kale
3. Beetroot stems
4. Swiss chard
5. Grassweed Powder
