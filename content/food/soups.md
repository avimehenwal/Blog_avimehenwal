## What is a Soup?


* Primarily a liquid food, usually hot/warm
* Vegetables + stock
* Similar to **stews**

$$ soup(s) = vegetables + stock $$

## How to make it?

### Vegetable Soup

1. 3 small potatoes
2. 2 onions
3. baby carrots
4. Rosemary and Garlic Powder
5. Soup setting on instant pot (20 mins)
